AvgSpeed Camera Zone Tracker

Privacy Policy — Last updated 25 March 2026

Overview

AvgSpeed Camera Zone Tracker is a GPS-based speed awareness app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

What data does the app access?

The app accesses your device's GPS location to calculate your speed and detect nearby average speed camera zones. This is the core functionality of the app.

Is my data stored or transmitted?

No. All GPS data is processed locally on your device in real time. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share your location data or any other personal information. No data leaves your device. There are no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

What about user-created zones?

If you record a custom speed camera zone, that data is saved locally on your device only. It is not uploaded or shared with anyone.

What about settings?

Your app preferences (speed unit, warning threshold, etc.) are stored locally on your device using standard device storage. They are not transmitted anywhere.

Third-party services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.
